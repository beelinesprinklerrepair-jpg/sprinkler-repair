New Sprinkler System Installation — Treasure Valley, Idaho

The Treasure Valley is one of the fastest-growing regions in the country, and Beeline has been installing new residential irrigation systems here since 2015. We work with builders putting in new construction homes and with homeowners who are adding irrigation to an existing property for the first time. Either way the approach is the same — we design the system for your specific lot, install it correctly with quality components, and walk you through everything before we leave.

Idaho averages about 12 inches of rain annually. Without irrigation, a Treasure Valley lawn doesn't survive summer. A properly designed and installed sprinkler system is one of the best investments you can make in your property here.

New Construction Sprinkler Installation

If you're building in Nampa, Meridian, Caldwell, Star, Kuna, or anywhere else in the Treasure Valley, the right time to install irrigation is before sod goes down. Trenching is cleaner, the grade is already set, and getting the system in before landscaping saves time and money. We work directly with general contractors and builders to coordinate irrigation installation as part of the construction timeline. We've done it across hundreds of new builds in the valley.

Existing Home Irrigation Installation

Adding irrigation to an existing yard requires more planning than a new construction install. We assess your lot, map zone coverage for your turf and bed areas, design for your specific water pressure, and install the system with minimal disruption to your existing landscaping. Most existing home installs in the Treasure Valley are completed in one to two days.

What's Included in Every Installation

  • On-site property assessment and zone layout design
  • Rotor heads for turf zones, spray heads for smaller areas and beds
  • Electric zone valves with individual shutoffs for each zone
  • Irrigation controller installation and full programming
  • Backflow prevention device installation
  • Pressure testing before backfill
  • Full system test and zone-by-zone walk-through at completion

Sprinkler System Design for Idaho

Treasure Valley soil is heavy clay in most areas — it absorbs water slowly and can pool or run off if zones are programmed to run too long. We design zone run times and precipitation rates around Idaho soil conditions, not generic national defaults. Your system will be set up to actually water your lawn efficiently from day one.
