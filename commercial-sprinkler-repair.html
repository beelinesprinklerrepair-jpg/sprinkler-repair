Commercial Sprinkler Repair — What We Fix

Commercial systems have the same failure points as residential systems, just more of them. Common commercial sprinkler repair work we handle across the Treasure Valley:

  • Broken or clogged rotor heads, pop-up spray heads, and specialty commercial heads
  • Valve failures — zones that won't turn on, zones that run continuously, solenoid replacements
  • Wiring faults and field wire breaks — common in older commercial systems
  • Controller diagnosis and replacement — commercial-grade Rain Bird, Hunter, and Orbit controllers
  • Mainline and lateral line leaks — locating and repairing underground breaks
  • Backflow device testing, repair, and replacement — required annually in Idaho
  • Low pressure diagnosis across large multi-zone commercial systems
  • Post-winter damage repair — cracked heads, split lines, and freeze damage from Idaho winters

Why Licensed Matters on Commercial Jobs

On a commercial property, an unlicensed contractor is a liability risk. If a repair creates a water damage claim, or a backflow issue contaminates a water supply, your insurance needs to see a licensed, insured contractor on file. Beeline holds the proper Idaho state contractor registration and carries full liability coverage on every commercial job we do. That's not optional — it's how we protect the property owners and managers we work with.
