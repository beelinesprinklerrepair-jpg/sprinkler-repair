What We Repair on Commercial Properties

We work on all major commercial irrigation brands — Rain Bird, Hunter, Toro, and Orbit. Whether it's a 6-zone HOA common area or a 40-zone office park, we have the equipment and experience to diagnose and repair it. Commercial parts go on the truck so most calls don't turn into two-trip jobs.

  • Broken or misdirected rotor heads and spray heads — full replacement matched to your existing system
  • Valve failures — zones that won't turn on, won't shut off, or run intermittently
  • Solenoid failures and wiring faults causing unresponsive zones
  • Multi-station commercial controller issues — lost programming, dead outputs, full diagnostics
  • Main line and lateral line breaks — including excavation and repair on larger commercial lines
  • Backflow device testing, certification, and replacement to meet municipal compliance requirements
  • Pressure problems across large multi-zone systems
  • Freeze damage after Idaho winters — cracked heads, split lines, valve damage

Backflow Compliance on Commercial Properties

Many cities in the Treasure Valley require annual backflow testing on commercial irrigation systems. Beeline tests, certifies, and replaces backflow prevention devices on commercial properties and submits the required documentation. If your property is past due on backflow certification, call before the water district sends a notice.
