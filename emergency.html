We Make Room for Real Irrigation Emergencies

Most sprinkler repairs can be scheduled during normal business hours. But when a line breaks, a valve will not shut off, or water is actively flooding your yard or getting close to your foundation, you need help as soon as possible.

Beeline offers emergency sprinkler repair visits and urgent irrigation service calls throughout Boise, Meridian, Nampa, Caldwell, Eagle, Kuna, Star, and Middleton. If you have an active irrigation leak or a sprinkler problem causing immediate damage, submit the form below and contact us directly.

We cannot guarantee a technician is available every time an emergency comes in. But when water is running and damage is happening, we will look for a way to work you into the schedule as quickly as we reasonably can.

When to Request an Emergency Visit

  • ✓A system that will not shut off and keeps running
  • ✓A broken line or valve actively flooding your yard
  • ✓Water pooling near your foundation or getting into your home
  • ✓An active problem that cannot safely wait for a normal appointment
  • ✓A failed valve stuck open that you cannot stop manually
