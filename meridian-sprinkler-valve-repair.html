Zone Valve Repair

A sprinkler zone not opening in Meridian means one section of your yard is going dry while the rest gets water. During peak summer heat, dead grass can show up within days on zones that aren't activating. The most common causes are solenoid failure, a broken wire between the controller and the valve, or a clogged diaphragm port that prevents the valve from lifting.

Our diagnostic approach is to test the wiring first. We connect a multimeter to the wiring at the valve and check for correct voltage when the controller activates that zone. If voltage is present and the valve still won't open, we pull the solenoid and inspect the diaphragm directly. This tells us exactly what's failed before we replace anything.
