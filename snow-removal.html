Common Questions

Snow Removal FAQ

What most commercial property managers ask us before signing.

Do you do residential snow removal or just commercial?
Our primary focus is commercial properties — parking lots, commercial buildings, HOAs, and multi-unit complexes. Contact us if you have a residential need and we can discuss what's possible.
What's included in a seasonal contract?
Seasonal contracts typically include all plowing events above your trigger depth, sidewalk clearing, and deicing materials. The exact scope is customized to your property. You'll know exactly what's included before you sign.
What's a "trigger depth" and how does it work?
A trigger depth is the snow accumulation that triggers service — for example, "plow when accumulation reaches 2 inches." We set this with you in advance based on your needs and liability requirements.
How quickly do you respond after a storm?
We monitor weather 24/7 and aim to have commercial properties cleared before normal business opening hours. Response times are guaranteed in your service agreement.
Are you insured for damage or accidents on my property?
Yes. We carry general liability and commercial auto insurance on every job. You'll receive a certificate of insurance before we start work.
When should I book for next winter?
Late summer is ideal — we fill routes and finalize equipment needs in August and September. Waiting until November means you may not get a slot. Call us now to lock in your contract for the upcoming season.
