Every Property in Your Portfolio Is Different

We've serviced enough commercial properties to know that no two are the same — even when they're managed by the same company. Zone counts vary. Some properties have older systems with deferred maintenance. Some have mixed head types from repairs done over the years by different contractors. Some are newer with documentation, others have no records at all.

We don't try to fit every property into the same service box. When we bring on a property management account, we start by learning what's there — walking each property, documenting the system, and identifying what needs attention. From there, we put together a service plan that reflects what the properties actually need, priced to the volume of work your portfolio represents.

System Mapping for Commercial Properties

🗺️ We Map What's There

One of the most common problems property management companies run into with irrigation is the lack of documentation. Systems get repaired, heads get moved, zones get added — and nobody writes it down. When a new PM takes over or a contractor shows up without context, you're starting from scratch every time.

Beeline offers system mapping as part of our commercial property management service. We document valve locations, zone counts and coverage areas, controller settings, backflow device locations, and any known issues. That map lives with the property file and gets updated as we work on the system. It's not a complicated product — it's just the documentation that should exist but usually doesn't.
