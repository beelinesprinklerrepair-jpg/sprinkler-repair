Common Problems We Fix

Sprinkler Issues We Run Into Across the Treasure Valley

Click on what matches your problem. We'll show you what's likely going on and how we fix it. We handle all of these every week across Treasure Valley.

💦 Broken or Misdirected Sprinkler Heads

Head not popping up, spraying sideways into the street, or watering the driveway? Sprinkler head replacement is one of our most common calls across Treasure Valley. We match heads to your system's zone pressure so they water right.

→

🚿 Zone Won't Turn On or Won't Shut Off

Almost always a valve or solenoid issue. Could be a worn diaphragm, failed solenoid, or a wiring problem. We diagnose the actual cause of the irrigation valve failure, not just replace parts at random.

→

💧 Wet Spot in the Yard When Sprinklers Are Off

Standing water between cycles usually means a valve is bleeding through or there's a cracked irrigation pipe underground. We find the sprinkler leak and fix it before it gets worse and drives your water bill up.

→

🌿 Brown or Dry Patch That Won't Green Up

A dead spot means part of the lawn isn't getting water. Could be a clogged nozzle, bad spray pattern, damaged head, or a pressure problem. We find and fix it.

→

📟 Sprinkler Controller Not Working Right

Lost programming, zones not running on schedule, or the display is dead? We diagnose and repair or replace irrigation controllers from all major brands including Rain Bird, Hunter and Orbit.

→

📉 Low Water Pressure on One Zone or the Whole System

Low sprinkler pressure points to a main line issue, a partially closed valve, or a regulator problem. We trace it to the source and fix the actual cause instead of guessing.

→

🔩 Backflow Device Leaking or Due for Testing

Nampa, Meridian and Boise all require annual backflow testing. We test, repair and replace backflow preventer assemblies and handle the city certification paperwork.

→

Not sure what's wrong? Just call or send us a note and we'll figure it out together.

Questions & Answers

Sprinkler Repair FAQ

The questions we hear most often.

How much does sprinkler repair cost?
Most basic sprinkler repairs in the Treasure Valley run between $100 and $300. Bigger repairs like underground line breaks, manifold replacements, or irrigation controller upgrades may cost more. We always tell you the price before we start. No work begins until you approve it.
Can you fix my sprinklers in one visit?
Yes, in most cases. We carry the parts most Treasure Valley irrigation systems need on the truck. If you can describe the problem and send photos when you schedule, we can arrive prepared and usually finish the same day.
What brands do you work on?
All of them. We service every major residential irrigation brand and system type found across the Treasure Valley — rotor systems, spray zones, drip irrigation, and smart controllers.
My sprinkler system won't shut off. What do I do?
Call us or request emergency service right away. An irrigation system that won't shut off is different from a standard sprinkler repair. We prioritize these calls. In the meantime, you can shut off your main irrigation valve or the water supply to stop the flow.
What cities do you serve?
We repair sprinkler systems in Nampa, Meridian, Boise, Caldwell, Eagle, Kuna, Star, and Middleton.
