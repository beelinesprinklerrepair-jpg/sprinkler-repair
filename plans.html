What is included in every plan

Every plan covers the full seasonal cycle for your irrigation system. Spring turn-on in March or April, monthly visits through the season to catch anything that needs attention, and a full compressed air blowout in the fall before the first freeze. You also get moved to the front of the line when you call — plan customers get priority scheduling over one-time service calls.

Most homeowners on a plan spend less over the year than they would calling us out twice for individual repairs. The spring turn-on alone catches freeze damage early before it turns into a bigger problem. The fall blowout protects the whole system through winter. The monthly visits keep small issues from becoming expensive ones.

  • Spring system activation and full zone inspection
  • Head adjustments and coverage corrections each visit
  • Minor valve and timer adjustments included
  • Fall compressed air blowout before the freeze
  • Priority scheduling — plan customers go first
  • One point of contact, same crew, all season

Plans run on a 12-month commitment billed monthly. We serve all eight Treasure Valley cities — Nampa, Boise, Meridian, Caldwell, Eagle, Kuna, Star and Middleton. Not sure which plan fits your system? Call (208) 880-2712 and we will help you figure it out.
