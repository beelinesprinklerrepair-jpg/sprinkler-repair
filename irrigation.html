Irrigation Repair — Treasure Valley, Idaho

Irrigation repair covers everything from a single broken head to a failed valve, a wiring fault, or a controller that won't respond. Beeline diagnoses irrigation problems accurately and fixes the actual cause — not just the symptom. We've worked on every type of residential irrigation system in the Treasure Valley and carry the parts most common systems need right on the truck, so most irrigation repair calls don't require a second visit.

Common irrigation repair problems we handle every season:

  • Broken or clogged irrigation heads not popping up or spraying correctly
  • Irrigation valve failures — zones that won't turn on or won't shut off
  • Wiring faults and solenoid failures causing unresponsive zones
  • Irrigation controller issues — lost programming, dead outputs, display failures
  • Low pressure on one zone or the entire irrigation system
  • Irrigation line leaks causing wet spots or soggy areas in the yard
  • Freeze damage from Idaho winters — cracked heads, split lateral lines

Irrigation Installation — New Construction & Existing Homes

A properly designed irrigation installation is one of the best investments you can make in a Treasure Valley property. Idaho's clay-heavy soil, combined with the region's low annual rainfall, means your lawn depends on a correctly designed and installed irrigation system to survive summer. Beeline designs irrigation installations for your specific lot — accounting for your soil type, water pressure, zone count, and landscaping layout.

We handle irrigation installation for two types of projects:

  • New construction irrigation installation — we work with builders and homeowners to get the system designed and installed before sod goes down, which is always cleaner and more cost-effective than retrofitting later
  • Existing home irrigation installation — adding a full irrigation system to a property that doesn't have one, including careful trenching to minimize disruption to your existing landscaping

Every irrigation installation Beeline completes includes properly sized irrigation zones, electric valves with individual shutoffs, an irrigation controller fully programmed for Idaho's seasonal watering needs, a code-compliant backflow prevention device, and a complete zone-by-zone walk-through before we leave.

Why Hire a Licensed Irrigation Contractor

Irrigation systems are connected to your home's water supply. In Idaho, work on those systems should be done by a licensed contractor. An unlicensed repair can void homeowner's insurance coverage, create code violations, and leave you responsible for damage that a licensed, insured company would have covered. Beeline is fully licensed and insured on every irrigation repair and irrigation installation job we do across the Treasure Valley. That's not a marketing line — it's how we protect our customers.
