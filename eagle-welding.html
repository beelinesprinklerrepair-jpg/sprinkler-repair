Do you offer mobile welding everywhere in Eagle?

Yes. We run mobile welding calls across all of Eagle — downtown Eagle, the State Street corridor, Banbury Meadows, Eagle Island, and the custom-home and foothills properties out west of Highway 16 and along the Boise River. If the project can be moved, shop service is also an option.

It depends on the job — material, access, and how much weld time is involved. The fastest way to get a real number is to text photos of the project and your Eagle location to 208-409-0756. You get a straight answer before any work starts.

Can you build a custom gate or railing for my Eagle home?

Yes. Custom gate welding and hand rail welding are a natural fit for Eagle's custom homes and estate properties — driveway and entry gates, ornamental railings, stair rails, and matching fence work. We build to your design or work from a look you like, mobile or in the shop.

Can you fix my trailer at my house or job site?

Yes. Trailer frames, ramps, hitches, fenders, and axle mounts are some of our most common mobile calls in Eagle — including horse and utility trailers on acreage and riverside property. We come to your driveway, barn, or job site so you are not towing a broken trailer across town.

Do you weld aluminum and stainless steel in Eagle?

Yes. We do clean TIG and MIG work on stainless and aluminum — railings, boxes, tanks, food equipment, trailers, and specialty projects, mobile or in the shop.

Do handrails or structural welding projects in Eagle need permits?

Some do. Structural work and most commercial projects in Eagle can require permits or engineered specifications through the city's building department. We build to the spec you or your contractor provide and are happy to coordinate on code-driven projects.
