Privacy Policy

How Beeline Sprinkler Repair collects, uses, and protects your information.

Last updated: July 6, 2026

Who We Are

Beeline Sprinkler Repair is a locally owned sprinkler repair and irrigation company serving the Treasure Valley, Idaho. Our website is www.beelinesprinklerrepair.com. You can reach us at (208) 880-2712 or by mail at 5606 W Amity Rd, Nampa, ID 83687.

Information We Collect

When you fill out a quote request or contact form on our website, we collect the information you provide, which may include:

  • Your name
  • Phone number
  • Email address
  • Service type requested
  • Your city or general location

We only collect what you choose to give us. We do not collect payment information through our website.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information you submit solely to respond to your quote or service request. Specifically:

  • To call or text you back about your sprinkler repair or service inquiry
  • To schedule an appointment or provide a quote
  • To follow up if we haven't been able to reach you

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Form Submissions — Formspree

Our contact and quote forms are processed by Formspree (formspree.io), a third-party form service. When you submit a form, your data is transmitted to Formspree's servers before being delivered to us. Formspree has its own privacy policy available at formspree.io/legal/privacy-policy.

Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics (GA4) to understand how visitors use our website — things like which pages get the most traffic and how people find us. Google Analytics collects anonymized data including your approximate location, browser type, and pages visited. It does not collect your name or contact information.

You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Google Maps

Some pages on our site include an embedded Google Maps iframe showing our business location. When you view these pages, Google may collect data according to their own privacy policy at policies.google.com/privacy.

Cookies

Our website uses cookies only for analytics purposes (Google Analytics). We do not use cookies for advertising or to track you across other websites. Most browsers allow you to control or disable cookies through your browser settings.

Data Retention

We keep your contact information in our customer records so we can continue to serve you in the future — for example, to schedule seasonal services, follow up on past repairs, or reach out when relevant. If you'd like your information removed from our records, just contact us and we'll take care of it.

Your Rights

You have the right to ask what personal information we have about you and to request that we delete it. To make that request, call us at (208) 880-2712 or send a letter to 5606 W Amity Rd, Nampa, ID 83687.

Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed at children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we'll update the date at the top of this page. Continued use of our website after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
