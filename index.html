What we weld

One call for repairs,
builds, and breakdowns.

From trailer welding repair to structural welding on commercial jobs, Idaho Welding Service handles welding and fabrication for homeowners, commercial contractors, farmers, and equipment owners.

01

Trailer weld repair

Frames, axles, ramps, hitches, fenders, service beds, and structural repairs that get you safely back on the road.

02

Heavy equipment welding

Heavy equipment and farm equipment welding repair — buckets, attachments, cracked mounts, worn steel, and field reinforcement.

03

Mobile welding

On-site welding services for equipment and projects that are too large, broken, or inconvenient to bring to a shop.

04

Custom fabrication

Custom metal fabrication — brackets, mounts, frames, modifications, and one-off builds made the way you actually use them.

05

Stainless & aluminum welding

Stainless steel and aluminum welding with clean TIG and MIG welding services — tanks, boxes, food equipment, trailers, and railings.

06

Hand rail welding

Gate and fence welding repair, plus handrails, guardrails, stairs, and residential or commercial steelwork built to fit the space.
