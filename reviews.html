Why Treasure Valley Homeowners Choose Beeline

Beeline has been doing sprinkler repair in the Treasure Valley since 2015. We are not a franchise, not a national call center, and not a company that subcontracts work to whoever is available that week. You call, you talk to a real person, and the people who show up are the ones who actually do the work. That has not changed since we started.

One thing customers mention regularly in their reviews is how quickly we get to them. We are not scheduling you two weeks out for a broken zone. Most jobs get on the calendar within a day or two, and a lot of repairs get done in a single visit because the trucks are stocked with the parts that show up most often - Rain Bird, Hunter, Toro, Irritrol, and Orbit heads, valves, solenoids, and fittings. If we can diagnose and fix it same day, we do.

The Treasure Valley is not just one place - it is eight cities with different water systems, different soil, and different installation eras. Nampa has pressure irrigation from the Nampa and Meridian Irrigation District that runs all summer at canal pressure. Caldwell has older homes with systems that date back 25 or 30 years and use hardware you do not see on new builds. Meridian subdivisions off Ustick and McMillan were largely installed during the construction boom and often have coverage gaps or overloaded zones. Eagle has a mix of new construction and HOA-managed systems. Boise's North End has mature landscaping, big trees, and PVC pipe that has been through a lot of Idaho winters. We have worked on all of it.

Pricing is straightforward. We diagnose first, then tell you what it will cost before any work starts. There are no surprise charges when the job is done. If something is minor and does not need repair right now, we will tell you that too. A lot of our repeat customers in Kuna, Star, and Middleton have stuck with us for years because of that approach.

We work on all major irrigation brands. Rain Bird and Hunter are the most common in the Treasure Valley, but Toro, Irritrol, and Orbit systems are everywhere too - especially in older Caldwell and Nampa properties. Whatever your system runs, we have the parts and the experience to fix it without telling you everything needs to be replaced.

Our Approach to Sprinkler Repair

Every job starts the same way: we run all your zones, watch what happens, and diagnose before we quote. You get a clear answer on what is wrong and what it will cost to fix it. No guesswork, no open-ended estimates. For most repairs in Boise, Meridian, and the surrounding cities, that is a conversation that happens in the first 20 to 30 minutes of the visit.
