Common Questions

Christmas Light Installation FAQ

Answers to what we hear most often.

Do you provide the lights, or do I need to buy my own?
We provide everything — all lights, clips, wiring, timers, and materials are included. You don't need to buy or own anything.
When should I book? Is it too early to call now?
The earlier the better. We start booking in mid-summer and fill up fast by September. Calling now gets you the best scheduling windows, best pricing, and first access to custom décor options. Don't wait until October.
What if something stops working mid-season?
Mid-season maintenance is included at no extra charge. If a bulb burns out, snow pulls something down, or anything stops working, just let us know and we'll come out and fix it promptly.
Do you install lights on commercial properties?
Yes. We handle residential homes, commercial buildings, HOAs, and businesses across the Treasure Valley. Large or complex projects just need a little extra lead time.
What holidays do you cover — just Christmas?
We do all of them. Christmas is our biggest season, but we also do Valentine's Day, St. Patrick's Day, Easter, Independence Day, Halloween, and more. Ask us about seasonal lighting packages.
What cities do you serve in the Treasure Valley?
We serve Nampa, Boise, Meridian, Eagle, Kuna, Caldwell, Star, and Middleton. Call us if you're outside these areas — we can often accommodate.
