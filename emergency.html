We Make Room for Real Sprinkler or Irrigation System Emergencies

Most sprinkler repairs can be scheduled during normal business hours. But when a sprinkler line breaks, a valve will not shut off, or water is actively flooding your yard or getting close to your home, you need help as soon as possible.

Beeline offers emergency sprinkler repair visits and urgent irrigation service calls throughout Boise, Meridian, Nampa, Caldwell, Eagle, Kuna and the surrounding Treasure Valley. If you have an active irrigation leak or a sprinkler system problem that is causing immediate damage, submit the form below and contact us directly.

We cannot guarantee that a technician is available every time an emergency comes in. But an irrigation emergency visit is handled differently than a regular sprinkler repair appointment. When water is running and damage is happening, we will look for a way to work you into the schedule as quickly as we reasonably can.

When to Request an Emergency Visit

  • ✓Emergency sprinkler system repair for a system that will not shut off and keeps running
  • ✓Urgent irrigation leak repair for a broken line or valve flooding your yard
  • ✓Emergency sprinkler service visit when water is pooling near your foundation or getting into your home
  • ✓After hours irrigation repair for an active problem that cannot safely wait for the next available appointment
  • ✓Sprinkler valve emergency repair when a failed valve is stuck open and you cannot stop the water
