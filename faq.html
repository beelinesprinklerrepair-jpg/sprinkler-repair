When should I schedule my spring turn-on?
Most Treasure Valley homeowners should schedule their spring activation between late March and early May, after the last hard freeze risk has passed. We book up fast in spring so call early to get on the schedule.
What happens if I skip the fall blowout?
Water left in irrigation lines freezes and expands over winter. PVC pipe, rotor bodies, valve diaphragms and fittings can crack or split. You won't find the damage until you turn the system on in spring and by then it can be an expensive repair. A blowout is always cheaper than the alternative.

Why is my sprinkler valve box full of water?
A full valve box is usually caused by a bad solenoid or a worn valve diaphragm. When the solenoid fails it can stick open and allow water to leak through constantly. A bad diaphragm does the same thing — when the rubber wears out or tears, water bleeds past it even when the zone is off. Call (208) 880-2712.
